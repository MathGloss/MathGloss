Let $\mathscr{A}$ be a category.  A map $X \stackrel{f}{\longrightarrow} Y$ in $\mathscr{A}$ is **epic**    (or an **epimorphism**)    if for all objects $Z$ and maps $\parpairi{Y}{Z}{g}{g'}$, $$ g \circ f = g' \circ f \implies  g = g'. $$
