Let $\mathscr{A}$ be a category.  A map $X \stackrel{f}{\longrightarrow} Y$ in $\mathscr{A}$ is **monic**    (or a **monomorphism**)    if for all objects $A$ and maps $\parpairi{A}{X}{x}{x'}$, $$ f \circ x = f \circ x' \implies  x = x'. $$
