A good example is the scalar product (dot product), which is a bilinear map $ \begin{array}{ccc} \reals^n \times \reals^n &\to &\reals \ (\mathbf{u}, \mathbf{v}) &\mapsto &\mathbf{u}.\mathbf{v} \end{array} $ of real vector spaces.
