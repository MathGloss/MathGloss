 A **split coequalizer** diagram consists of maps
$ \xymatrix{ x \ar@<.5ex>[r]^f \ar@<-.5ex>[r]_g & y \ar[r]^h \ar@/^3ex/[l]^t & z \ar@/^/[l]^s}$ so that $hf = hg$, $hs=1_z$, $gt=1_y$, and $ft = sh$.
