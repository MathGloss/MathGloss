A **norm** on a [vector space](https://mathgloss.github.io/MathGloss/vector_space) $V$ is a real-valued functional $||\cdot||$ such that for all $v,w \in V$ and $\alpha \in \mathbb R$, the following properties hold:
1. $||v+w|| \leq ||v|| + ||w||$;
2. $||\alpha v|| = |\alpha| \cdot ||v||$;
3. $||v|| \geq 0$ and $||v|| = 0$ if and only if $v=0$.
