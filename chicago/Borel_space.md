A **Borel space** is a [measurable space](https://mathgloss.github.io/MathGloss/chicago/measurable) $(X, B)$ given by the [Borel σ-algebra](https://mathgloss.github.io/MathGloss/chicago/borel_σ-algebra) on $X$.
