A [binary operation](https://defsmath.github.io/DefsMath/binary_operation) $\cdot$ on the set $A$ is **associative** if for all $a,b,c \in A$, the following relationship holds: $$a\cdot (b\cdot c) = (a\cdot b)\cdot c.$$
