Let $V$ be a [vector space](https://mathgloss.github.io/MathGloss/chicago/vector_space). A subset $K\subset V$ is **convex** if for all $x,y \in K$ and $t \in [0,1]$, the vector $tx + (1-t)y$ is in $K$.
