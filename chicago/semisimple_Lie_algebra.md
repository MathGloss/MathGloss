A [Lie algebra](https://mathgloss.github.io/MathGloss/Lie_algebra) $L$ is **semisimple** if its [radical](https://mathgloss.github.io/MathGloss/radical_of_a_Lie_algebra) $\text{Rad}(L) = \{0\}$.
