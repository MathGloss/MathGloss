A **right coset** of the [subgroup](https://mathgloss.github.io/MathGloss/subgroup) $H$ of the [group](https://mathgloss.github.io/MathGloss/group) $G$ for the element $x\in G$ is the set $Hx = \{hx\mid h \in H\}$.
