The **transpose** of an $m\times n$ [matrix](https://mathgloss.github.io/MathGloss/matrix) $A = (a_{ij})$ is the $n\times m$ [matrix](https://mathgloss.github.io/MathGloss/matrix) $A^T$ whose entries are given by $a_{ji}$.
