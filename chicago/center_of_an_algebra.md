The **center** of an [algebra](https://defsmath.github.io/DefsMath/algebra_over_a_field) $A$ over the [ring](https://defsmath.github.io/DefsMath/ring) $R$ is the set $Z(A) = \{z\in A \mid za=az \text{ for all } a\in A\}$.
