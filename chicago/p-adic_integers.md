The **$p$-adic integers** is the subset of [Qp](https://mathgloss.github.io/MathGloss/chicago/p-adic_field) defined by $$\mathbb Z_p = \{x\in \mathbb Q_p\mid {\vert}x{\vert}_p \leq 1\}.$$
