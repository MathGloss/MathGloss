An **edge path** in a [graph](https://mathgloss.github.io/MathGloss/chicago/graph) $X$ is a finite composite of [oriented edges](https://mathgloss.github.io/MathGloss/chicago/oriented_edge) $k_n$ with $k_{n+1}(0) = k_n(1)$.
