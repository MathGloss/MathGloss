A [field](https://mathgloss.github.io/MathGloss/chicago/field) $k$ is **algebraically closed** if every non-constant [polynomial](https://mathgloss.github.io/MathGloss/chicago/polynomial_ring) in $k[t]$ has a root in $k$.
