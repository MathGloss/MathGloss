A **rectangle** $Q$ in $\mathbb R^n$ is a product of [intervals](https://mathgloss.github.io/MathGloss/chicago/interval) in $\mathbb R$: $$Q = [a_1,b_1]\times \cdots\times [a_n,b_n].$$
