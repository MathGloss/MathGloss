The **principal part** of a [Laurent series](https://mathgloss.github.io/MathGloss/chicago/Laurent_series) $$f(z) = \sum_{n=-\infty}^\infty a_n(z-c)^n$$ is the series of terms of negative degree: $$\sum_{n=-\infty}^{-1}a_n(z-c)^k.$$
