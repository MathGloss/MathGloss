Let $U \subset \mathbb R^n$ be [open](https://mathgloss.github.io/MathGloss/open). A function $f:\mathbb U \to \mathbb R^n$ may be written as an $m$-tuple of functions $f_i:U\to \mathbb R$ so that $$f=(f_1,\dots,f_m).$$
