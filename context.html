Category Theory in Context

Category Theory in Context is an introductory book about category theory by Emily Riehl. A link to the book can be found here. More information about the author can be found here.

We are very grateful to the author for her permission to use the book for this project!

Corpus info

There were 493 concepts identified from the formatting in the book (i.e. definition blocks, boldfacing), from which we were able to find 315 mappings to Wikidata. Of these, 108 were duplicates bringing us to a total of 206 unique concepts in Wikidata.
