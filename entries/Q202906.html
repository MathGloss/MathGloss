MathGloss Definition

A homeomorphism between two topological spaces $X$ and $Y$ is a function $f: X\to Y$ such that 1. $f$ is continuous. 2. $f$ is bijective. 3. $f^{-1}:Y\to X$ is continuous.

Two topological spaces $X$ and $Y$ are homeomorphic if there exists a homeomorphism between them.
