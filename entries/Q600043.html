Character theory

MathGloss Definition

Let $(V,\rho)$ be a finite-dimensional representation of a group $G$ where $V$ is a vector space over the field $F$. The character of this representation is the function $\chi_\rho:G\to F$ given by $\chi_\rho(g) = \text{Tr}(\rho(g))$.
