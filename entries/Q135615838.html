rank of a module

MathGloss Definition

Let $R$ be a ring and let $M$ be a free $R$-module. Then the cardinality of a basis for $M$ is the rank of $M$.

We can also define the rank for modules that are not free: it is the maximum number of linearly independent elements of $M$.
