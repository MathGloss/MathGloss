Laplacian

MathGloss Definition

Let $D\subset\mathbb R^2$ be connected and open and let $f:D\to \mathbb R$ be of class $C^2$. Then the Laplacian of $f$ is given by the sums of the second partial derivatives $$\Delta f(z) = f_{xx}(z) + f_{yy}(z).$$
