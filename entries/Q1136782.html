Lie algebra representation

MathGloss Definition

A representation $\phi$ of a Lie algebra $L$ is a homomorphism to the general linear algebra ($\phi:L \to \mathfrak{gl}(V)$) where $V$ is a vector space. Here we require the dimension of the vector space underlying $L$ to be finite, but admit arbitrary dimension for $V$.
