essential supremum

MathGloss Definition

Let $(X,\Sigma, \mu)$ be a measure space. The essential supremum $\alpha$ of a function $f:X\to \mathbb R$ is the smallest real number $\alpha$ such that $\mu(\{x\mid f(x) > \alpha\}) = 0$.
