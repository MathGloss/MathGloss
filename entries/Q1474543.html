Jacobian determinant

MathGloss Definition

Let $U\subset\mathbb R^n$ be an open subset and let $g: U \to\mathbb R^n$ be differentiable. Then Jacobian determinant of $g$ is the function $Jg:U\to\mathbb R$ given by the absolute value of thedeterminant of the Jacobian matrix of $g$: $$Jg(x)= \vert \text{det}(Dg(x))\vert .$$
