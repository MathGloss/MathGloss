Linear Algebra/Subspace

MathGloss Definition

Let $V$ be a vector space. A subset $W \subseteq V$ is a vector subspace if for all $\alpha \in F$ the ground field and $u,v \in W$, $\alpha v \in W$ and $u+v \in W$.
