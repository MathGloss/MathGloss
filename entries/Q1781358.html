wedge sum

MathGloss Definition

Let $(X,x_0)$ and $(Y,y_0)$ be based topological spaces. The wedge sum $X\vee Y$ is the quotient of the disjoint union $X\amalg Y$ given by identifying $x_0$ and $y_0$ to a single point.
