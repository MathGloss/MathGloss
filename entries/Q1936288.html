total variation

MathGloss Definition

Let $(X,\Sigma)$ be a measurable space with $\mu$ a signed measure. The total variation of $\mu$ is the function $\tau$ given by $$\tau(A) = \pi(A) + \nu(A)$$ where $\pi$ is the positive variation of $\mu$ and $\nu$ is the negative variation of $\mu$.
