Minor

MathGloss Definition

Let $V$ be a vector space and let $T: V\to V$ be a linear transformation with corresponding matrix $A$. The matrix coefficients of the matrix corresponding to the exterior product $\wedge^m(T)$ of $T$ are called the minors of $A$.
