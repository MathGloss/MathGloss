analytic function

MathGloss Definition

A function $f:\mathbb U\to \mathbb C$ is analytic on the open set $U\subset\mathbb C$ if for any $z_0\in U$, there exists a power series $$f(z) = \sum_{n=0}^\infty a_n(z-z_0)^n$$ with $a_n \in \mathbb C$ for all $n$ that converges to $f(z)$ in a neighborhood of $z_0$.
