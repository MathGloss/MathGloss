splitting field

MathGloss Definition

Let $F$ be a field and let $f$ be a monic polynomial with coefficients in $F$. Then a field extension $F\subset E$ is a splitting field if there exist $\{a_i\}_{i=1}^d$ such that $$f(t) = \prod_{i=1}^d (t-a_i) \in Et]$$ and moreover $E$ is [generated by these $a_i$, i.e. $E= F(a_1,\dots, a_d)$.
