Kronecker delta

MathGloss Definition

The Kronecker delta $\delta_{ij}$ is a function of the two variables $i$ and $j$ defined by $$\delta_{ij} = \begin{cases}0 & \text{if }i\neq j \\ 1 & \text{if } i=j.\end{cases}$$
