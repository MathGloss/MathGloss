Quotient space (linear algebra)

MathGloss Definition

Define an equivalence relation $\sim$ on a vector space $V$ with subspace $W$ by $$v_1 \sim v_2 \text{ if } v_1-v_2 \in W.$$ Then the quotient of $V$ by $W$ is the set of equivalence classes of $\sim$ in $V$.
