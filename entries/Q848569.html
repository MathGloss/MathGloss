complete metric space

MathGloss Definition

Let $X$ be a metric space. The completion of $X$ is the complete metric space $(\tilde X, \tilde \rho)$ such that $X$ is a dense subset of $\tilde X$ and $\rho(x,y) = \tilde\rho(x,y)$ for all $x,y \in X$ as constructed here.
