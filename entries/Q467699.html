line integral

MathGloss Definition

A complex-valued function $f:[a,b]\to\mathbb C$ is integrable if the component functions $f(t) = u(t)+iv(t)$ are Riemann integrable as real-valued functions. The complex integral is then given by $$\int_a^b u(t)\text dt + i\int_a^b v(t)\text dt$$
