inverse matrix

MathGloss Definition

The inverse of an $n\times n$ matrix $A$ is an $n\times n$ matrix $A^{-1}$ such that the products $$AA^{-1} = A^{-1}A = I_n$$ where $I_n$ is the $n\times n$ identity matrix.
