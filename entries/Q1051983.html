cyclotomic polynomial

MathGloss Definition

Let $p$ be a prime. Then the $p$th cyclotomic polynomial is the polynomial over $\mathbb Z$ whose roots are the primitive $p$th roots of unity. It is denoted $\Phi_p (x) = x^{p-1} + x^{p-2} + \cdots + x + 1$.
