MathGloss Definition

A subset $H\subset G$ of the group $G$ is a subgroup of $G$ if it is itself a group under the operation inherited from $G$.

The subgroup axioms are

  • closure under $*$;
  • the identity element of $G$ is in $H$;
  • for every $h\in H$, the inverse element $h^{-1}$ is also in $H$.
