group representation

MathGloss Definition

Let $V$ be an $n$-dimensional vector space. A representation of a group $G$ is a group homomorphism $\rho: G \to GL(V)$ from $G$ to the $n$-dimensional general linear group of invertible linear transformations of $V$.
