Radon measure

MathGloss Definition

Let $X$ be a Hausdorff topological space and let $(X, \Sigma, \mu)$ be a measure space where $\Sigma$ is the Borel σ-algebra on $X$. The measure $\mu$ is a Radon measure if it is inner regular and locally finite.
