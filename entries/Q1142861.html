Direct sum of modules

MathGloss Definition

Let $\mathfrak g$ and $\mathfrak h$ be Lie algebras. The direct sum $\mathfrak g \oplus \mathfrak h$ of $\mathfrak g$ and $\mathfrak h$ is the vector space direct sum of $\mathfrak g$ and $\mathfrak h$ when considered as vector spaces endowed with the bracket $$[(X_1,X_2),(Y_1,Y_2)]=([X_1,Y_1],[X_2,Y_2]).$$
