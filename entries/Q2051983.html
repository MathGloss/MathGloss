Adjoint matrix

MathGloss Definition

The conjugate matrix transpose of a complex $m\times n$ matrix $A$ is the $n \times m$ matrix $A^*$ given by taking the usual matrix transpose $A^T$ and then setting $A^*$ to be the matrix whose entries are the complex conjugates of each entry of $A^T$.
