Wikidata ID
Q11861938
Label
weak convergence

MathGloss Definition

Let $X$ be a normed linear space with dual space $X^*$. A sequence $\{x_i\}_{i\in\mathbb N}$ in $X$ converges weakly to $x \in X$ if $\lim\limits_{i\to\infty} f(x_i)=f(x)$ for all $f\in X^*$.
