MathGloss Definition

The radius of convergence of a power series $$f(x) = \sum_{n=0}^\infty a_n(x-c)^n$$over a Banach algebra with norm $\vert \cdot\vert $ is a real number $r$ such that the series converges to $f(x)$ for all $\vert x-a\vert <r$.
