MathGloss Definition

The unitary group $\text{U}(n)$ is the group of $n\times n$ unitary matrices together with the operation of matrix multiplication.

This group is a matrix Lie group because it is a subgroup of the general linear group $\text{GL}(n,\mathbb C)$.
