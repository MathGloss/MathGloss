elementary matrix

MathGloss Definition

Let $A$ be a matrix and let $A_i$ be the $i$th row of $A$. A row operation on $A$ is any of the following operations: 1. Switch two rows 2. Multiply a row by a nonzero constant 3. Add one row to another
